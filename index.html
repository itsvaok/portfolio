About Me

I'm a Fine Arts major at the University of Cincinnati, in the DAAP (Design, Architecture, Art, & Planning) program, pursuing a career in Graphic Design or any Art related feild. I create logos, fliers, stream overlays, and other digital art.

Each piece is designed to feel intentional and full of personality. I also create drawings and paintings, bringing the same attention to detail and creativity to traditional media.

Every piece I make is designed to communicate clearly and creatively, using color, composition, and typography.
